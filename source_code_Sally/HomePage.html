The activities of the training program "Curricula for the Future and Future Studies" were launched.

Under the patronage of the Minister of Education, Dr. Tayseer Al-Nuaimi, the activities of the training program "Curricula for Forward-Looking and Future Studies", organized by the Ministry in cooperation with the Arab Planning Institute and the Ministry of Planning and International Cooperation, kicked off, and its activities will continue until the 18th of this month.

2.Education ministry emphasizes the availability of public safety conditions and requirements in its schools

The Ministry of Education called on its directorates in the field to ensure that the requirements and conditions for public safety in schools are available during the winter, in order to preserve the safety of students and teachers.
