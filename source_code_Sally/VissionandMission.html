Ministry of Education Jordan

Vission and Mission

Vision

Entrepreneurial, belonging, participatory educational community
committed to values, its approach to science and excellence, to the
universality.

.

Mission

Providing equal opportunities to obtain a high-quality education that
enables the learner from critical scientific creative thinking, team work,
lifelong learning, and providing skills and values ​​to be active citizens
belonging to their homeland contributing to the elevation of the world
and humanity.

Main Values

Freedom, justice and equality, good citizenship, belonging, moderation,
respect for opinion and other opinion, transparency,
responsibility, leadership, and building effective partnerships.
