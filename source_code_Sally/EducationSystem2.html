Education System in Jordan

Education philosophy and objectives:


The philosophy of education in the Kingdom is derived from the Jordanian constitution, the Arab-Islamic civilization, the principles of the Great Arab Revolution and the Jordanian national experience. This philosophy is represented in the following foundations:

Intellectual foundations:


  1. Faith in God Almighty Faith in the ideals of the Arab nation.
  2. Islam is a behavioral intellectual system that respects a person and exalts the position of the mind and encourages knowledge, work and creation.
  3. Islam is an integrated value system that provides the valid values ​​and principles that form the conscience of the individual and the community.

Foundation of nationalism and national humanity


  1. Faith in God Almighty Faith in the ideals of the Arab nation.
  2. The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan is an Arab country and its system of government is a hereditary monarchy and loyalty to God, the nation, and the king Jordan is part of the Arab world and the Jordanian people are an integral part of the Arab and Islamic nation.
  3. The Jordanian people are an integrated unit and there is no place for racial, regional, sectarian, tribal or family intolerance. The Arabic language is an essential pillar in the existence of the Arab nation and a factor in its unity and revival.
  4. The Arabic language is an essential pillar in the existence of the Arab nation and a factor in its unity and revival.
  5. Adhering to the Arab identity of Palestine and all the usurped parts of the Arab world and working to restore it.
  6. The Palestinian issue is a crucial issue for the Jordanian people, and the Zionist aggression against Palestine is a political, military, and cultural challenge for the Arab-Islamic nation in general, and Jordan in particular.

Educational stages and objectives

Kindergarten stage for two years at most. The basic education stage is for ten years. Secondary education for two years.
